The Independence Assumption Requires Four Sequential Resolutions
The scenario in which private credit, the refinancing wall, and the stagflation trap resolve independently requires four distinct conditions to hold in sequence. Each has a documented break point. None is currently on the required timeline.
Condition 01
Break point confirmed
A ceasefire reopens Hormuz and removes the oil shock
Why this breaks
Even a ceasefire tomorrow produces a minimum 85–110 day flow normalisation before tanker backlogs clear. The IEA 6-month restoration estimate is structurally wrong: built for a single strait, single resolution model. The two-clock model establishes that Hormuz and Bab el-Mandeb operate on independent timelines with no synchronisation mechanism.
HFI Research tanker backlog analysis · Two-clock model published 30 March 2026 · IEA structural critique
Condition 02
Depends on Condition 01
Break point confirmed
The Fed cuts into a slowing economy, restoring the monetary cushion
Why this breaks
The stagflation trap is operative. Every $10 sustained move higher in oil raises the terminal rate by approximately 25–50 basis points. March NFP +178,000 against consensus 60–65,000. The dollar went flat on a significant upside surprise: markets are pricing a Fed that is politically constrained and operationally paralysed. Rate cuts require the oil shock to resolve first — which requires Condition 01.
Stagflation Trap SAB, March 2026 · NFP data BLS April 2026 · Robin Brooks dollar regime change analysis
Condition 03
Depends on Conditions 01 + 02
Break point confirmed
Leveraged borrowers refinance the maturity wall at acceptable rates
Why this breaks
The 2021–2022 vintage cohort borrowed at 2–3%. Even with meaningful Fed easing, refinancing clears at 4.5–5.5% minimum — a doubling of debt service costs for the weakest borrowers. The basis trade is already unwinding: approximately 700,000 contracts in leveraged short Treasury futures retired since August 2024, $500bn DVP repo contracted since November 2024. The cheap funding conditions are gone independently of Fed policy.
Corporate Bond Rollover Wall SAB, August 2025 · Adler basis trade unwind data · NY Fed primary dealer reports
Condition 04
Depends on Conditions 01 + 02 + 03
Break point confirmed
Private credit funds meet redemptions from recovered portfolio valuations
Why this breaks
Private credit portfolio valuations are mark-to-model, not mark-to-market. Recovery requires borrower EBITDA to recover, which requires Condition 03. In the interim, pension funds carrying up to 15–20% private credit exposure face simultaneous private credit write-downs and public bond portfolio pressure — with fixed liabilities, actuarial review cycles, and no central bank put available. The forced seller appears one layer up from where the 5% cap provides containment.
Private Credit opacity brief, October 2025 · Ares, Apollo, Blue Owl Q1 2026 gating data · Edition 26 pension fund analysis, February 2026
Four conditions. Four documented break points. Each condition depends on the resolution of the one before it. The independence assumption does not require any one of these to fail. It requires all four to hold simultaneously. The data from Q1 2026 suggests none of them are on the required timeline.
