Europe enters the 2026 refill season at 29.55% — the lowest April level on record for the third consecutive year. To reach the EU's 90% regulatory target by November, approximately 61 percentage points of injection capacity are required, against a backdrop of disrupted Qatari LNG supply and accelerating Russian gas phase-out.
