Price discovery strength is illustrative based on market structure analysis. In a normally functioning market, each link transmits the benchmark signal with minimal friction. Under current conditions — tanker backlog of 900+ vessels, war risk insurance at 3.75% of insured value vs 0.1% pre-war, Cape of Good Hope rerouting adding ~$930k fuel per voyage — each link adds cost, delay, and uncertainty that the benchmark price does not capture.
