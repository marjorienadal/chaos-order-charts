Four Clocks

The dependency chain — why Clock 4 gates everything

Clock 4 is the falsification test. If mines remain uncleared, Clocks 1–3 cannot resolve regardless of what the diplomatic calendar produces.

Clock 4 The Mines
Gates all Unknown timeline
The constraint
Laid haphazardly with no central coordination and no complete mapping. Some have drifted from original positions. Neither Iran nor the US has rapid mine clearance capability.
Why it gates
Mines create an insurance classification problem independent of diplomatic status. A ceasefire removes intent-based threat. It does not remove drifting mines with no operator and no off switch. Uncertainty itself is the constraint — underwriters price undetected mine risk, not confirmed mine count.

Clock 2 War Risk Insurance
6–8 weeks after Clock 4
The constraint
3.75% vs 0.1% pre-war. Joint War Committee has not delisted the area. P&I clubs vote independently on quarterly committee cycles. The 1988 Tanker War precedent: 3–4 months from ceasefire date, not first clean transit.
Why it gates
Ships cannot commercially transit until insurance normalises. The gap doesn't compress gradually — it compresses in steps as individual clubs complete review cycles. Some clubs move faster, creating fragmented period where some vessels can transit and others cannot.

Clock 1 Tanker Backlog
85–110 days after Clock 2
The constraint
900+ vessels stuck in or around the Gulf. Clearing the queue requires sequential transits through a 34-nautical-mile corridor. This is a logistics problem, not a diplomatic one.
Why it gates
Even if Hormuz opened today, the queue does not dissolve on a press conference. The 85–110 day estimate assumes the other three clocks have already resolved. It is the last constraint in sequence, not the first.
Clock 3 Rerouting Costs
Parallel with Clock 2
Cape of Good Hope adds 16–32 days and ~$930k in fuel per voyage. Hapag-Lloyd: no normal network for 6–8 weeks even if ceasefire holds. Rerouting economics normalise in parallel with insurance reinstatement — but both depend on Clock 4 resolving first.
