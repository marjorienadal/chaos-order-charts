Shanghai Gold Premium During Stress Events

Premium Over LBMA London Pricing ($/oz)

2013 Gold Crash

Premium spiked to $20 as Asian buyers accumulated physical during Western paper market sell-off. Divergence between paper and physical demand.

March 2020 COVID

Premium hit $35 due to logistics disruption and panic buying. Brief spike, quickly normalized as transport resumed.

2022-2023 Structural Shift

Post-Russia freeze, premium remained structurally elevated ($15-25) even during calm periods. Western banking stress (Mar 2023) pushed it to $45.

Pattern Recognition:

  • Pre-2022: Premiums spike during specific events, then return to $3-8 arbitrage range
  • Post-2022: Premiums remain structurally elevated at $15-25, spiking to $45 during Western financial stress
  • Key Insight: Premium widens specifically during Western banking/financial system stress, not Chinese domestic factors. This signals Eastern buyers paying extra for physical metal outside dollar system during periods of Western financial vulnerability.
