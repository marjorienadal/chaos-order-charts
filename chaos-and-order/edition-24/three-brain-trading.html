The Three-Brain Trading System

Separating Analysis, Execution, and Emergency Overrides

1
The Analyst
Pattern Recognition & Thesis Development
Function:
  • Reads Strategic Advisory Briefs, commodity reports, central bank statements
  • Identifies structural patterns
  • Builds investment theses
  • Assigns conviction: High/Medium/Low

What it doesn't do: Decide timing, execute trades, or override positions emotionally

2
The Systematist
Rules-Based Execution & Risk Management
Function:
  • Takes Brain 1's thesis and conviction
  • Executes position sizing based on risk parameters
  • Implements systematic risk management
  • Follows rules without emotion

Position Sizing:
High conviction (0.8-1.0): 5-10% risk
Medium (0.5-0.7): 2-5% risk
Low (0.2-0.4): 0.5-2% risk

3
The Override
Emergency Exit Authority
When It Fires:
  • Market microstructure breakdown
  • Liquidity disappearing (spreads exploding)
  • "Everybody's in this trade" moment
  • Physical delivery stress indicators

Protocol:
Document observation immediately â†’ Check if emotion or pattern recognition â†’ Execute override â†’ Post-analysis validation
