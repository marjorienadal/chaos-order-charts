How Containment Becomes Contagion
The 5% quarterly cap contains the gating event at the fund level. It does not contain what happens one layer up. Once LP stress activates, it closes a self-reinforcing loop that the cap was not designed to interrupt.
Entry point
→
Private credit funds gate at 5% quarterly cap. Pension fund LP receives partial redemption. Mark-to-model valuation masks full deterioration. LP return assumption now structurally unachievable.
Stage 01
LP Stress
Pension fund faces write-downs on private credit allocation. Fixed liabilities unchanged. Return assumption gap widens. Actuarial review cycle forces recognition. Political pressure mounts toward further allocation cuts.
→
LP reduces allocation, pulls forward redemptions
Stage 02
Credit Tightening
Private credit funds face sustained redemption pressure beyond the 5% cap. New deployment slows. Existing borrowers cannot refinance or extend. Terms tighten on rolling facilities. Covenant enforcement increases.
↑
Portfolio marks down further. LP stress compounds.
Self-
reinforcing
loop
↓
Credit unavailable at viable rates for weakest borrowers
Stage 04
Compounded LP Stress
Pension fund portfolio now carries deteriorating private credit, stressed public bonds, and a return gap that contribution increases alone cannot close. The forced seller is no longer theoretical. It is the institution with fixed liabilities and no exit.
←
Default and restructuring events force portfolio recognition
Stage 03
Borrower Stress
2021–2022 vintage borrowers cannot refinance at viable rates. PIK toggles and amend-and-extend mechanics delay but do not prevent defaults. EBITDA deteriorating under energy input cost and demand compression simultaneously.
Why the 5% cap does not interrupt this loop
The cap was designed to prevent a run on a single fund. It was not designed for a scenario in which the LP base is simultaneously under pressure from a separate source — the corporate refinancing wall and the stagflation trap — that removes the central bank cushion that would otherwise allow orderly absorption. Containment at the fund level is not containment at the system level when the LP itself is the transmission mechanism.
